Who is Akira Toriyama ?

Akira Toriyama (鳥山明, Toriyama Akira ? ) is a Japanese manga author and character designer born on April 5, 1955 in Nagoya in Aichi Prefecture and died onMarch 1 , 2024 as a result of a subdural hematoma .
He is the creator of the famous Dragon Ball , a work which enjoys worldwide success.
In 2007 , the Japanese ranked it third "best manga of all time" on the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the Japan Media Arts Festival , behind Slam Dunk and JoJo's Bizarre Adventure 1 .
In 1981 he won the Shogakukan Manga Award for best shōnen manga with Dr. Slump , which sold over 35 million copies in Japan.
It was adapted into a successful anime series, with a second anime premiering in 1997 , thirteen years after the manga ended.
Its next series, Dragon Ball , became one of the most popular manga in the world.
Having sold between 250 and 300 million copies worldwide, it is one of the best-selling manga of all time , considered one of the main reasons for the period when manga circulation was at its peak between the mid- 1980s and mid- 1990s .
Abroad, the cartoon adaptations were more successful than the manga and helped to boost the popularity of these cartoons in the Western world.
